ABOUT AMAZONIA

The brand AMAZﾃ年IA is about all the sensory experiences related to the tropical rainforest. The sound of birds and waterfalls, the feeling of thick leaves beneath the feet, the smell of humid air and fragrant flowers, the sight of colorful nature and the play of light and shadows.

AMAZﾃ年IA's purpose is to raise awareness and make people think about the value of the gifts of nature.

IMAGERY

Patterns are the key graphic elements to be applied across AMAZﾃ年IA touchpoints to show uniqueness, contrast and expression.
